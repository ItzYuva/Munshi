Keep your accounts by just texting.

Munshi is the friend who happens to be great with money. Tell it what you spent the way you’d text anyone — “chai 15”, “auto 50, lunch 120”, “aaj movie pe 300” — and it quietly notes every rupee, files it under the right category, and never forgets.

Ask how your month is going, set a budget and get a gentle nudge before you overspend, and receive a tidy report on the 1st. No apps to open, no spreadsheets to keep — just chat, in English, Hindi, or Hinglish.
